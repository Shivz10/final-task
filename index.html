About Us

This "W Trek" is for all you ladies out there who desires to break all the shackles of life in order to see a new you.We believe that the outdoors are right place to experience the inner leader hidden in you.The W Trek is specially designed for all those ladies who are either new to the beautiful city of Chandragiri or are skeptical to explore the Chandragiri all by themselves or go the Chandragiri Weekend Treks alone.
Once you are on board for this trek you will see a completely new perspective of not just outdoors but also of life.This will be your day when you can unleash the woman inside you and can also experience the beutiful world of outdoors.There is no difference between a man and a woman n the eyes of nature and to experience that,a weekend gateway forall you ladies can be the perfect beginning.As mountains never differentiates between the genders,hence neighther its beauty nor difficulty level increases or decreases.W Trek is a platform to begin a journey in the outdoors which you truly deserve.
